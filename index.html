Your Simple 4-Week Path

Week 1: Get Started with Claude

~4 hours

Learn what AI is and start using Claude for everyday tasks

  • Set up your free Claude account
  • Your first conversation with AI
  • Write emails and messages

Week 2: Get Better Results

~4 hours

Learn how to ask for exactly what you want

  • Writing clear requests
  • Getting better answers
  • Practice with real examples

Week 3: Use AI for Your Work

~4 hours

Apply AI to your actual work and projects

  • Research and summarization
  • Creating documents and presentations
  • Solving your specific problems

Week 4: Make It Your Own

~3 hours

Build your own AI workflow that works for you

  • Your personal AI toolkit
  • Time-saving shortcuts
  • Next steps and continued learning
