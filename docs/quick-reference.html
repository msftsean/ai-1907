Questions to Ask As You Learn

Phase 1:

  • What market intelligence could I find with Perplexity?
  • What data do I have that NotebookLM could analyze?

Phase 2:

  • Which workflow applies to my biggest pain point?
  • How could I chain these tools together?

Phase 3:

  • What are better questions to ask Perplexity?
  • How do I frame competitive research?

Phase 4:

  • What task repeats weekly that I could automate?
  • How much time would I save?

Your Week-by-Week Checklist

Week 1

  • ☐ Watch Grace Leung Perplexity + NotebookLM (20 min)
  • ☐ Watch Grace Leung NotebookLM Best Ways (15 min)
  • ☐ Take notes on how to apply to your business
  • ☐ Message Sean when done

Week 2

  • ☐ Watch 8 AI Workflows (17 min) - TWICE
  • ☐ Take detailed notes on each workflow
  • ☐ Identify 3 workflows that could help you
  • ☐ Think about which ONE to experiment with

Week 3

  • ☐ Watch Tina Huang playlists (AI for Productivity & Business AI)
  • ☐ Practice asking Perplexity better questions
  • ☐ Start Enovair videos
  • ☐ Decide on your ONE workflow experiment

Week 4

  • ☐ Finish Enovair videos
  • ☐ RUN YOUR EXPERIMENT (30-60 min max)
  • ☐ Document what you discovered
  • ☐ Message your brother with results
