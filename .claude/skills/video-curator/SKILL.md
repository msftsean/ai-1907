# Video Curator for Alpha AI Curriculum

## Target Audience

- Non-technical professionals ages 30-60+
- Business owners, ministers, corporate professionals, retirees
- People who want practical AI skills, not coding

## Video Selection Criteria

### Must Have
- Clear, easy to follow explanations
- Practical demonstrations (not just talking heads)
- Beginner-friendly language
- Under 30 minutes (ideally 15-20)

### Avoid
- Heavy coding/technical content
- Marketing/sales pitches disguised as tutorials
- Outdated content (check publish date)
- Low production quality

## Video Evaluation Checklist

1. [ ] Is the presenter clear and engaging?
2. [ ] Are there practical examples shown?
3. [ ] Is it accessible to non-technical viewers?
4. [ ] Is it recent (within last 6-12 months)?
5. [ ] Does it fit the curriculum week theme?

## Adding a New Video

1. Watch the full video first
2. Note key timestamps and topics
3. Write a module description (see content-writer skill)
4. Update both learning-plan.html and index.html
5. Test that the link works
